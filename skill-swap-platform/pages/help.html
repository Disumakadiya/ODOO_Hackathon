Frequently Asked Questions

ğŸš€ Getting Started

How do I get started on Skill Swap?

Getting started is easy! Simply create an account, build your profile with the skills you can offer and want to learn, and start browsing other users' profiles to find potential swap partners.

Is Skill Swap free to use?

Yes! Skill Swap is completely free to use. We believe in making skill sharing accessible to everyone.

ğŸ‘¤ Creating Your Profile

What should I include in my profile?

Include your name, location (optional), skills you can teach others, skills you want to learn, and your availability. Adding a profile photo helps others recognize you!

Can I make my profile private?

Yes! You can choose to make your profile private. Private profiles are only visible to users you've already connected with.

How do I add or remove skills?

Go to your profile page and edit your skills list. You can add multiple skills separated by commas, and update them anytime.

ğŸ’Œ Making Swap Requests

How do I request a skill swap?

When you find someone with skills you want to learn, click "Request Swap" on their profile. Choose which skill you'll offer in return and send them a message.

What happens after I send a request?

The other user will receive your request and can either accept, reject, or ask for more information. You'll be notified of their decision.

Can I cancel a request I sent?

Yes, you can delete pending requests that haven't been accepted yet. Go to your "Swap Requests" page to manage them.

ğŸ“‹ Managing Requests

How do I respond to incoming requests?

Go to your "Swap Requests" page to see incoming requests. You can accept, reject, or ask questions before deciding.

What if I need to reschedule a swap?

Contact your swap partner directly to reschedule. Communication is key to successful skill swaps!

How do I rate my swap experience?

After completing a swap, you'll be prompted to rate your experience. This helps build trust in our community.

How do I stay safe when meeting for swaps?

Always meet in public places, let someone know where you're going, and trust your instincts. Consider video calls for initial meetings.

What if someone behaves inappropriately?

Report any inappropriate behavior to our support team immediately. We take safety seriously and will investigate all reports.

Are there age restrictions?

You must be 18 or older to use Skill Swap. We recommend parental guidance for users under 18.

How can I contact support?

You can reach our support team through the contact form or email us directly at support@skillswap.com

What information should I include in my support request?

Include your username, a clear description of the issue, and any relevant screenshots or details to help us assist you quickly.

How quickly will I get a response?

We aim to respond to all support requests within 24 hours during business days.
